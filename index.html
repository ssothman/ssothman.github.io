Research

I study Active Matter, I am focusing on the characteristics of Active Brownian Particles under the effect of alignment field. We found that the critical point has shifted due to the effect of the alignment field.
We also researched some of the critical exponents and correlation functions, and have found out that it belong to the same exponent family.
And now we are focusing on the dynamics of ABPs inside complex geometeries such as Y, T, and inverted Y junctions.
In parallel, I am studying the dynamics and behavior of mixture of ABPs under the effect of an alignment field.
